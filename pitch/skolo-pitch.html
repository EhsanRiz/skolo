Fee management and school communication — built for Lesotho and South Africa

The problem

Most schools in Lesotho and low-fee South African markets track fees in exercise books or shared Excel files. Chasing arrears is done by word of mouth. There is no easy way for parents to check their balance, and bursars spend hours every term reconciling who has paid and who hasn't — time that should be spent supporting learners.

What Skolo does

💰
Smart fee ledger
Set fee plans by grade. Generate monthly or termly entries automatically. Track paid, partial, overdue in real time.
🎒
Learner management
Register learners with guardian contacts. Import from Excel. Every learner gets a unique reference number.
🧾
Instant receipts
PDF receipt generated and downloaded the moment a payment is recorded. No manual paperwork.
📱
Parent portal
Parents check their child's balance via a secure link. No app, no login — just a link sent by the school.
📋
Export to Excel
One click exports the full monthly ledger with totals — ready for the bursar's report to the principal.
📅
Events & announcements
School calendar + bulk announcements to parents. SMS integration available.
